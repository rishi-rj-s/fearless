DUMBBELLS

Dumbbells come in different shapes. Can be used for performing exercises like the renegade row, dumbbell push-up, and dumbbell burpees.
Suitable For: Home gyms, beginners, advanced lifters, and people recovering from injuries.
Price: $27-429 (15 - 55 pounds)

×

BARBELLS

Barbells come in different shapes and sizes. Specialized bars are designed to alleviate stress from your joints and better focus on the target muscles.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and home gyms.
Price: $30-800

×

KETTLEBELL

A kettlebell is a cast-iron or cast-steel ball with a handle attached at the top. Kettlebells are a great tool for cardiovascular, strength, and flexibility training. They are especially great for you if you're into functional training.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and home gyms.
Price: $13-300 (5-75 pounds)

×

BENCH PRESS

The bench press is one of the first exercises most newbies learn to perform. You could use the bench for the bench press or other exercises like the dumbbell pullover, incline dumbbell row, seal row, etc.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and home gyms
Price: $89-349

×

POWER RACK

The power rack, also known as the full rack, has a walk-in design and allows you to train different body parts. Excercises you can perform on the power rack include squat, bench press, bent-over row, pull-up, and rack pull.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and commercial gyms..
Price: $263-1,500

×

LEG PRESS

The leg press is an incredibly effective machine for building lower body strength and muscle mass. Since this gym equipment is usually bulky, it is best suited for commercial gyms.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters, and commercial gyms.
Price: $499-2,495

×

HACK SQUAT

Although incredibly effective, the conventional barbell squat is not for everyone. You could also get a multi-purpose hack squat machine that transforms into a leg press machine.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and commercial gyms.
Price: $1,200-3,000

×

SMITH MACHINE

The Smith machine is versatile gym equipment that can be used for multiple exercises. The multiple hooks of this machine add an aspect of safety to your training regimen. You could rack the barbell at one of the hatches when you're about to hit muscle failure.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and commercial gyms.
Price: $699-2,000

×

WEIGHT BENCH

The bench is a versatile piece of training equipment that is available in gyms around the world. If possible, you should prefer an adjustable bench that can be used as a flat, incline, or decline bench.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters, home and commercial gym.
Price: $48-339

×

HIP ABDUCTOR/ADDUCTOR MACHINE

Weak abductors and adductors are common reasons behind lower body injuries. Use moderate weights, follow a full range of motion, keep the reps slow and deliberate, and contract the life out of your muscles with every rep.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and commercial gyms.
Price: $1,100-2,700

×

FUNCTIONAL TRAINER

The functional trainer is often confused with the cable crossover machine. Since the functional trainer is more compact, it is a better fit for home gym setups.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters, home and commercial gyms.
Price: $1,517-2,999

×

CABLE ROW MACHINE

The cable row machine is an incredibly effective machine to target your lats and middle back. If you don't have the budget or space for it in your home gym, you could perform the seated cable row on a cable crossover or functional trainer machine.
Suitable For: Beginners to advanced lifters and commercial gyms.
Price: $800-4,500
